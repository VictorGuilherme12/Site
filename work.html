Fuel Fury is a competitive racing game that delivers an exhilarating experience with its Drift, Circuit, and Open World modes. In this game, players race for fantastic rewards, including amazing cars and substantial sums of money, continually enhancing their machines for peak performance. In the Drift mode, players defy the laws of physics, sliding through corners with precision and flair, while the Circuit mode demands consistency and strategy to maintain position and clinch victory in the final lap.

Welcome to Simple World, a multiplatform MMORPG for mobile and desktop. Dive into guild events featuring PvP and PvE quests, explore epic dungeons, and take on World Bosses for great rewards. The game also includes territorial wars, global events, and a battle royale mode. With three races and six classes, you can customize your character's stats to fit your playstyle. Quests take you across varying landscapes, enriching your gaming experience.

Meet Joy! A clever and headstrong squirrel who falls into a mystical world and seeks his way back home, but he realizes it won't be that easy. With the help of two magical entities, he will encounter a variety of creatures, face challenging obstacles and boss battles. Joy will learn the meaning of things, experiencing a journey of deconstruction, self-knowledge and important lessons about friendship, courage, perseverance and much more.
